Robo

The main part of the city's population. They have anthropomorphic robotic bodies consisting of limbs on flexible joints. Some of them have modifications to suit the activity or decoration. Everyday clothing is made of synthomaterial and follows simple geometric shapes.
The official language of communication is Xi.

About us

We are a young creative team which wants to throw our non-trivial ideas into the world of art. At the moment, our priority is to write a book about life in Zird which consists of robots and where fantasies about a mechanical society, their peculiar routine, conflicts, views, aspirations and confrontations, and of course, unexpected events are intertwined

Not everything that seems true is not true:)

Each of you can support us and express your desire to learn this fascinating story. Robo, Andro, as well as their cute robo-pets will evolve and go their way with you, acquiring a unique story and weaving their threads with each other into strong knots of common destiny.
